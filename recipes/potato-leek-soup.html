Potato Leek Soup

Sopa de Poro y Papa

Description

This is a creamy, rich soup that is popular in Mexico City. In the warmer months it is typically served chilled, but works great as a hot soup for those cold winter nights.

Ingrediants

  • 4 tablespoons butter

  • 3 medium leeks, white and light green parts only, roughly chopped

  • 2 medium white onios, roughly chopped

  • 1 medium russet potato, peeled and roughly chopped

  • 8 cups chicken stock

  • Salt and fresh ground black pepper, to taste

  • 1/2 cup crema or sour cream

  • Olive oil, for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a 4-qt. saucepan over medium heat.Add leeks, onions, and potato, and cook, stirring often, until soft, about 20 minutes.

  2. Add the stock and cook, stirring occasionally, until the potato is very tender, about 35 minutes.

  3. Use an immersion blender and pur√©e until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. (see note)

  4. To serve, drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil and a spoonful of crema, or sour cream.

Note

The immersion blender makes this so much easier to be able to do it all in one pot, but you CAN do this with a blender if you dont have one. Just be sure it's safe to use with hot liquids.
